FAQ

Quick Answers, Before You Pick Up the Phone

How much does fridge repair cost in Bangalore?

Repair pricing depends on the brand, model, and the exact fault — we share a fixed-rate quote before starting any work, so there are never any surprises. Visit fee is just ₹250, and it’s fully waived the moment you approve the repair.

Do you repair all fridge brands?

Yes. We service LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, Godrej, Haier, Bosch, Siemens, Panasonic, Hitachi, Electrolux, Voltas, IFB and most other single-door, double-door, side-by-side and French-door refrigerators — including inverter and smart models.

How fast can you reach my home?

In most areas of Bangalore we reach within 90 minutes of your call. Our phone lines and emergency technicians are available 24 × 7, every day of the year — including late nights, Sundays and public holidays.

Do you offer a warranty on the repair?

Yes — every repair carries a 1-year warranty on the service and 6 months on parts replaced. If the same issue returns within that window, we fix it free. You get a signed warranty card on the spot.

Are the spare parts genuine?

Yes. We use only OEM or manufacturer-approved spare parts and provide a bill with part numbers so you can verify. We’ll never fit a generic compressor, PCB, or gasket without your informed approval.

What happens if my fridge can’t be repaired?

If your fridge is economically unrepairable (usually a rare sealed-system failure on a 12+ year old unit), we tell you honestly and charge only the ₹250 visit fee. No upsell, no pressure.
